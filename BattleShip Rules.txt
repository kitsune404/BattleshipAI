Board: 10 x 10 board labeled [a-j] and [1-10]

Ships:
Carrier: 5 Spaces
Battleship: 4 Spaces
Cruiser: 3 Spaces
Submarine: 3 Spaces
Destroyer: 2 Spaces

Each player has 2 versions of the game board. The top board contains
the locations of the misses (white) and hits (red) made against the other
player. The bottom board contains the player's ships and the hits the opponent
has made against the ships.
